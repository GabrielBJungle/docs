Genesis is the first NFT collection from Jungle.

These assets are built on artificial scarcity (Capped Supply: 1,000) and have economic utility in the form of yield.

They are designed to deliver value to long-term holders through the issuance of new assets in future phases, premier access to exclusive features and games across the Jungle ecosystem, exclusive rewards, points yield and more.

It's going to be distributed through a free mint process.
