## We like adrenaline.

Whether in a 1v3 clutch in the last round of a ranked match, trading memecoins with high volatility and a dream of 100x, or playing a mini FTW championship in the office worth 10$ in SOL on Phantom with the rest of the team divided in the crowd.
We want to reproduce this adrenaline in all the activities we promote within the Jungle ecosystem. We are creating experiences that we ourselves would want to participate in and that would be fun for people like us.
To create this, we deviate from the norm, we have our own flavor.
