Jungle is a Web3 Mobile Gaming publisher established in Q4 2022, after securing a $6M Seed Round led by Bitkraft, Framework & Delphi.
Building For The Win (FTW), a lightweight mobile RPG shooter, launching Q4 2023.
Jungle's ecosystem connects four pillars: Mobile Games, Gamified Community, Onchain Fun & Crypto Incentives. It's being built to drive value back to users through shared economics.
