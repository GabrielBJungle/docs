We are embarking on creating an ecosystem with a clear focus on user co-ownership and engagement. Our initiative aims to democratize the gaming experience by providing users with a stake in the ecosystem's development and success. This approach is rooted in our belief in the transformative potential of web3 to enhance user agency and create a more inclusive and participatory digital environment.
Jungle's ecosystem is composed by the core pillars:
Mobile Games
Gamified Community
Crypto Incentives
Onchain Fun
