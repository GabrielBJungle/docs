While obtaining a Stake NFT, the holder will accumulate Spins & Super Spins.
When using spin, you randomly receive:
x Points [90% Chance]
x1 Summoning Scroll [10% Chance]
When using Super Spin, you randomly receive:
x Points
x1 Summoning Scroll (rarity variety)
