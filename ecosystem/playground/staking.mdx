Genesis NFT holders can stake their NFTs on the Playground platform.
While staked, each NFT will reward the holder as follows:
Every 24h: x1 Spin
Every 7d: x1 Super Spin
Rewards accumulate in the user's profile and can be redeemed whenever you want.
