The points program is designed to reward users for their contributions to the ecosystem. It is an offchain score built for accumulation, leaderboard evaluation and token distribution.
Participants in the Jungle ecosystem can earn and accumulate points by performing different activities that contribute to the growth and solidification of the ecosystem. Some sources of points include:
- Creating Content
- Referring Friends
- Engaging on Discord
- Playing Discord Minigames
- Playing Playground Minigames
- Playing FTW
- Staking NFTs
- and more
