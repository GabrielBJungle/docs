The points program is designed to reward users for their contributions to the ecosystem. It is an offchain score built for accumulation, leaderboard evaluation and token distribution.
Os participantes do ecosistema da Jungle podem ganhar e acumular pontos ao performarem diferentes atividades que contribuem para o crescimento e solidificação do ecosistema. Algumas fontes de pontos incluem:
Creating Content
Referring Friends
Engaging on Discord
Playing Discord Minigames
Playing Playground Minigames
Playing FTW
Staking NFTs
and more
