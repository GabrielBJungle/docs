Start staking your Genesis NFTs, earn Juice, and reap the rewards.

## **How**

* **Step 1:** Visit [https://app.itsjuice.xyz/](https://app.itsjuice.xyz/)

* **Step 3:** Select your Genesis NFTs and click “Stake”

*Unstaking won’t make you lose any accumulated Juice*
