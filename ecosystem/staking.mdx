Genesis NFT holders can stake their NFTs on the Playground platform to passively receive JUICE.

While staked, each NFT will reward the holder as follows:
- 100 JUICE / hour

Stake more NFTs to obtain massive JUICE multipliers as follows:
- x1 Genesis: +0% Juice Multiplier
- x3 Genesis: +15% Juice Multiplier
- x5 Genesis: +30% Juice Multiplier
- x7 Genesis: +30% Juice Multiplier
- x10 Genesis or more: +70% Juice Multiplier
