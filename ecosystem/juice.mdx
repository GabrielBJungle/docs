The Juice program is designed to reward users for their contributions to the ecosystem. It is an offchain score built for accumulation, leaderboard evaluation and token distribution.
Participants in the Jungle ecosystem can earn and accumulate Juice by performing different activities that contribute to the growth and solidification of the ecosystem. 

Some sources of Juice include:
- Genesis Staking
- Playing FTW Tournaments and custom matches
- FTW Wagering matches
- Playing FTW Clans Tournaments
- and more
