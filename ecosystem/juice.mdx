The Juice economy is designed to reward users for their contributions to the Jungle ecosystem. It is an offchain score built for accumulation, leaderboard evaluation and future token distribution.


Participants in the Jungle ecosystem can earn and accumulate Juice by performing different activities that contribute to the growth of the ecosystem.

Sources of Juice include:

* Genesis NFT Staking
* FTW Tournaments&#x20;
* FTW Wagering Engagement
* FTW Clans Membership
