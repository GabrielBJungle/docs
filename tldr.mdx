Jungle is a Web3 Mobile Gaming publisher, building mobile-first games at the intersection of Web3 and skill-based real money gaming on Solana.

For The Win (FTW) is our first mobile title, a lightweight mobile RPG shooter, currently in Global Early Access stage.

Jungle's ecosystem connects four pillars: Mobile Games, Gamified Community, Onchain Fun & Crypto Incentives.
